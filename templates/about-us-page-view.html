Our Vision

We are a team of professionals committed to corporate excellence. We aspire to develop high competency and achieve zero error in service through high technology and be synonymous high quality service.

Our History

Started in 1990, Speed Line Express Ltd a leading courier agency is pioneer among developing and organized and well-networked chain of courier services . Its founders Mr. Serazul Islam & Mr. Eaar Hossain spearheaded courier business and as of today their boasts of 22 years of experience in the field. Under their experience guidance, business has seen exponential growth and established their successful businessman.
